What Is Invisalign?

Invisalign is a revolutionary orthodontic treatment that replaces the uncomfortable and unsightly metal brackets and wires of traditional braces. The innovative clear acrylic aligners gently guide teeth into their correct position.

Invisalign eliminates the need to visit our office every month for uncomfortable wire tightening appointments. While certain foods become forbidden with traditional braces for fear of pulling a bracket loose or breaking a wire, patients can remove their Invisalign aligners for eating allowing them to eat anything they want.

Removable aligners also make cleaning teeth much easier. With no bands or wires required, patients no longer have to worry about cuts to the inside of their mouth. Most patients find the biggest advantage to be the virtual invisibility of the aligners, meaning nobody has to know they have them in except the orthodontist.
